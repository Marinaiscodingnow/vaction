Der Friesenhof

This family-run hotel on the North Sea island of Föhr is located just 1.5 km from the sandy beach. Friesenhof Nieblum features a wellness area, rental bikes, free WiFi in public areas and a quiet garden. All rooms are warmly decorated, featuring pinewood furnishings and carpeted floors. Breakfast is included in the price and is available in the hotel’s bright breakfast room.

Hotel Landliebe

Set in Nieblum, 2.4 km from Nieblum Beach, Hotel Landliebe offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and barbecue facilities. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

  • Link to Hotel Landliebe

Sternhagens Landhaus

Located in Oevenum, 3.7 km from Museum of local frisian history Wyk, Sternhagens Landhaus provides accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and luggage storage space, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

Food & Resturants

Insel Fohr offers a wide selection of resturants; but here were three of the ones that looked most promising. The island is known for the resturants's wonderful service and familiarity. However, no matter where you go, you'll get a good bite to eat.

Cafe Klein Helgoland

Enjoy the unique atmosphere with boats in the harbour, sheep on the dike and the vastness of the North Sea. It has a wind-protected terrace that provides a cosy ambience and offers you a unique view of the Wyker harbour and the Wadden Sea.

  • Link to Cafe Klein HelgoLand

Restaurant Zum Walfisch

A beautiful restaurant that balances modern and the culture of the island wonderfully. They offer a wide selection of delicious dishes in a cozy and relaxed atmosphere. They also provide a wonderful view and even better service.

  • Link to Restaurant Zum Walfisch

Klatt´s Gute Stuben

A cozy restaurant right in town, Klatt´s Gute Stuben is the perfect place for a drink. They also serve a wide varity of meals to dig into. The inside is positively stunning and truly eye-catching.
